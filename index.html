Observe

What do you
notice?

Gender rules are everywhere — in jokes, chores, freedoms, and fears. We start by seeing what usually goes unnoticed.

Question

Who decided
this?

Instead of telling students what is right or wrong, we ask questions. Who benefits? Who adjusts? Who stays silent?

Unseen rules

Not all rules are written.
Some are inherited, repeated and never questioned.

Everyday choices

Fairness lives in daily moments.
At home, school, friendships and play.

No blame

Systems, not individuals.
Understanding replaces defensiveness.
Act

What could
change?

Equality grows through small, believable actions — one sentence, one choice, one pause at a time.
