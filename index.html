Visual-first learning

Gender equality,
seen differently

We use visuals, questions and play to help young people notice invisible gender rules — and imagine fairer ones.

How learning unfolds

Nothing is taught as a lecture. Understanding grows by noticing, questioning and trying.

Observe

Students react to visuals and stories.
What feels normal? What feels unfair?

Question

We ask who decides and why.
Rules become visible, without blame.

Experiment

One small action to try.
Ideas move into real life.
