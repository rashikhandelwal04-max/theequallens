Grassroots problem

We listen to everyday rules kids grow up with.

The Equal Lens works at the root — homes, classrooms and peer spaces — turning unspoken gender rules into conversations kids can question.

How we work

Workshops that feel like play, not lectures.

Games, drawing, movement and discussion help students spot patterns they’ve never been asked to notice.

What shifts

Small pauses. Big questions.

We’re not trying to fix everything. Just helping young people pause before repeating a rule they never questioned.
